Our Story

Building a community of faith since 1985

Our History

Grace Community Church was founded in 1985 by a small group of families who shared a vision for a church that would be a welcoming place for everyone in our community. What began as a Bible study in a living room has grown into a vibrant community of faith serving thousands of families.

Over the years, we've seen God work in miraculous ways. From our first building project in 1992 to our recent sanctuary expansion in 2020, we've remained committed to our mission of helping people find faith, hope, and community.

Today, Grace Community Church is a diverse family of believers from all walks of life, united by our love for God and our commitment to serving our community. We continue to grow and adapt, always seeking new ways to share God's love in our changing world.

Our Mission

To lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ by creating environments where people are encouraged and equipped to pursue intimacy with God, community with insiders, and influence with outsiders.

Our Vision

To see our community transformed by the love of Christ, where every person experiences God's grace, finds their purpose, and uses their gifts to serve others.
