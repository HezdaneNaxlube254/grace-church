Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

When are your services?

We have two Sunday worship services at 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM. We also have a Wednesday prayer service at 7:00 PM.

What should I wear?

Come as you are! You'll see everything from jeans to suits. We care more about you than what you wear.

Do you have programs for children?

Yes! We have excellent children's programs for all ages during both Sunday services and Wednesday evenings.

How do I become a member?

We offer a New Members Class monthly. Contact the office or check our events page for the next class date.

How can I get involved in serving?

We have many opportunities to serve! Contact any ministry leader or attend our monthly "Discover Your Ministry" class.

Do you offer counseling?

Yes, our pastors offer pastoral counseling. Contact the office to schedule an appointment.
