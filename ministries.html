Our Ministries

At Grace Community Church, we believe everyone has a place to belong and grow. Our ministries are designed to help you connect with others, deepen your faith, and discover how God has uniquely gifted you to serve.

Adult Ministry

For: Adults of all ages and stages

Our adult ministry focuses on spiritual growth through Bible studies, small groups, and fellowship opportunities. Whether you're single, married, or retired, you'll find a place to connect.

Sundays at 10:00 AM | Wednesday Bible Study at 7:00 PM
Get Connected

Youth Ministry

For: Middle & High School Students (Grades 6-12)

A dynamic ministry where students can explore faith, build authentic relationships, and have fun in a safe environment. Weekly gatherings include worship, teaching, and small groups.

Sunday School at 10:00 AM | Youth Night: Fridays at 7:00 PM
Learn More

Children's Ministry

For: Children (Ages 0-5th Grade)

We create safe, engaging environments where children can learn about Jesus through age-appropriate teaching, worship, and activities. Our volunteers are trained and background-checked.

Sundays at 9:00 AM & 11:00 AM | Wednesday Kids Club at 7:00 PM
Learn More

Women's Ministry

For: Women of all ages

A community where women can grow spiritually, build meaningful friendships, and support one another through life's joys and challenges. Includes Bible studies, retreats, and special events.

Weekly Bible Studies | Monthly Fellowship Events
Join Us

Men's Ministry

For: Men of all ages

Helping men become godly leaders in their families, workplaces, and communities. Through Bible study, accountability groups, and service projects, we encourage each other to live with integrity and purpose.

Saturday Breakfast: 1st Saturday at 8:00 AM | Weekly Small Groups
Join Us

Outreach Ministry

For: Everyone with a heart to serve

Sharing God's love in practical ways through local service projects, mission trips, and community partnerships. We believe faith without works is dead, and we're committed to making a difference.

Monthly Service Projects | Quarterly Mission Trips
