About Our Church

Serving families in Nairobi with faith, compassion, and community since 2015

Our Mission

To share the transformative love of Christ through worship, discipleship, and compassionate service in our community.

Our Vision

A thriving, multi-generational church family that impacts Nairobi through faith in action and authentic community.

  • Joyful Generosity
