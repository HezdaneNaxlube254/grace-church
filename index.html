Our Church Family

Building community and growing in faith together since 2015

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, serve others, and transform communities through the Gospel.

Our Vision

To be a thriving, multi-generational church that impacts Nairobi and beyond with God's love and truth.

Our Values

Biblical teaching, authentic community, heartfelt worship, compassionate service, and joyful generosity.
